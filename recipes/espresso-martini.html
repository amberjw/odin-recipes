Espresso Martini Recipe

Description

Get your caffeine fix with this bold and boozy espresso martini recipe. Made with just 3 simple ingredients, youâ€™ll love this espresso martini for a fun cocktail at home. Stay energized!

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1 ounce Kahlua or a comparable coffee liqueur
  • 1 ounce brewed espresso, cooled.
  • coffee beans, optional garnish

Method

  1. Combine the espresso, vodka, and Kahlua in a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Shake until well mixed and cold.
  3. Strain the espresso martini into a cocktail glass.
  4. If desired, top with a dusting of espresso powder and coffee beans for garnish. Enjoy responsibly!
